Grand Circus

About

Grand circus has been founded in 1998. The history of grand circus can be traced since last century. We have a great variety of animals, more than 20 kinds from big mammals to small pets. We have earned more than 30 awards on best circus shows. And we rescued animals from all over the world. There are more than 10,000 visitors per year to watch our shows. Our tour will go to 10 countries this year in 2019. Look forward to seeing you in one of them!
